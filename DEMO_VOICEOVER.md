## Timing Breakdown:

- **Introduction**: 5 seconds
- **Onboarding**: 15 seconds (quick, essential fields only)
- **Meeting Input**: 20 seconds (show all 3 methods briefly)
- **Submit & Results**: 20 seconds (fast processing, show groups)
- **Total**: 60 seconds

## Tips for 1-Minute Demo:

1. **Fast pace**: Speak quickly but clearly - this is a rapid demo
2. **Skip details**: Don't explain every field, just show the flow
3. **Visual focus**: Let the UI do the talking - point out key features
4. **Smooth transitions**: Move seamlessly between sections
5. **Key highlights**: Emphasize "automatically", "intelligently", "multi-agent"
6. **Action speed**: Click through forms quickly, don't wait for every field
7. **Show, don't tell**: Demonstrate features rather than explaining them
